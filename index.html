About The author

Nathan Joblin

Nathan Joblin is COO + Co-Founder of Modern Wisdom Press. His passion for simplicity and all things operational has included decades of experience in the publishing, nonprofit, and educational sectors, where he has directed production, finance, and administration for a number of organizations.

Nathan's journey to becoming a published book author inspires him to support clients in overcoming the inherent obstacles to getting a book into the world. His expert skills and passion for streamlining the publishing process empower first-time authors to transform their manuscripts into bestselling books.

What's in the book?

Chapter 3: Healthier, Happier, and More Present

We were taught to be team players and play by the rules, and since we tend to do what we are told, we make sure that we are good at it. And because we play the game so well, we believe we should be rewarded with happiness, success, and prosperity. In reality, though, nothing is promised to any of us, and we do ourselves a disservice by defining success in material terms. This dynamic will continue indefinitely until we take responsibility for defining our own needs and desires in this world.
